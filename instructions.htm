Sample Queries

Query Details
[,5];[10,20];[50,] Find values less than or equal to 5 OR between 10 and 20 inclusive OR greater than or equal to 50.
TP53;BAX;AP0A1 Find values matching TP53 OR BAX OR APOA1

Saving complex queries

On the top left corner of the table, you'll see a string that looks like col1:[0,5]|col2:A;B;C... If you save this string, you can use it later to apply the same filters by pasting it into the append filters box in the navigation bar.

You can also save the results of a complex query by clicking the save (floppy) icon on the nav bar. This will give you a tab-demilited file of the filtered results.
